Fleet Network

The FleetNetwork is a network of droids that all work together as a collective mind to complete a goal. We have designed different types of droids for different tasks.

Our goal is to tidy up the planet, these droids will collect harmful things to the environment and put them places where they can be dealt with properly.

Using a centralized server the droids have an adaptive brain, meaning they can reposed to any situation thrown at them

We hope to keep expanding and make as much of a positive impact as possible.
